# Ruleset
## The first instance (alpha test one) will be played over the course of 3 weeks, asynchronously, and will be comprised of ten players. The game will be played on the provided GitHub repository.
1. Every week, each player must participate in the game by producing a valid article on a valid subject as vetted by the Gamemaster.
2. In any given week, a player can be relieved of their duty to participate if they give another player the duty to participate once more. A player cannot do this for two weeks in a row.
3. If a player cannot participate, they can instead submit a stub. Please don't do this unless you really are busy as fuck.
4. During the first week, the subject is freeform. A player can request to be tethered to a specific subject or area of subjects, chosen on their own accord or at the discretion of the Gamemaster.
5. For the first week, a player cannot participate more than once.
